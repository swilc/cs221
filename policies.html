Assignment Policies

Please read the following carefully!

Assignments are to be submitted through Microsoft Teams. Assignments will not be accepted any other way (e.g. email attachments).

Assignments must be submitted by 11:59 pm on the day they are due for full credit.
Late assignments are accepted for 80% credit (a 20% late penalty is applied).
No assignment will be accepted more than one week late. Pay attention to assignment close dates in Microsoft Teams.

Documentation is an integral part of projects. Writing is expected to be well-formatted and to adhere to college-caliber grammar, spelling, capitalization, and punctuation standards.
