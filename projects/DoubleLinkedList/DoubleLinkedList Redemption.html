Programming assignment follow-up: DoubleLinkedList Redemption

earn extra-credit for addressing test failures from the DLL project

Description:

Fix as many issues with your DoubleLinkedList as you can, as identified by the instructors' test suite results.

Your improved DLL will be retested and 75% of the difference between your original testing score and your improved testing score (rounded) will be awarded extra-credit points.

Note: The only part of the project being considered, here, is the functionality of your DLL as determined by running the instructors' test suite. Nothing else from the project (README, your ListTester class, etc.) will be reevaluated.

Example

Your original DLL passed 50% of the instructor tests (25/50 points). You work to address failed tests and your improved DLL passes 80% of the instructor tests (40/50 points). You receive (40-25)*0.75 = 11 points extra-credit.

Submission

After updating your IUDoubleLinkedList.java file, please replace the original file with your updated version in the DoubleLinkedList Project assignment in Teams and then resubmit the Teams assignment. Your new IUDoubleLinkedList.java file will be tested and your assignment grade will be updated.
